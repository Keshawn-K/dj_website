About DJ Myke

Welcome to the official website of DJ Myke!

DJ Myke is a passionate DJ and music producer based in Nairobi, Kenya. With many years of experience in the industry, DJ Myke had the privilege of performing at various venues and festivals.

DJ Myke's music style is a blend of different genres, creating a unique sound that resonates with audiences of all ages.
